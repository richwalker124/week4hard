Rules

Your goal is to beat all of the other characters with one! You select your character by clicking their portrait. You select your foe by selecting their portait after. You can select a new foe after you beat your current one. Each character has their own special ability as well! Roy deals double damage against characters without swords. Ridley has a one in ten chance of dealing 4 times damage! Cloud will deal double damage every 5 hits, and Incineroar will deal 1 extra damage for every point of missing health! You win when you beat the three characters that you didn't pick. Goodluck!

*** For those who are unaware, the characters from left to right: Roy, Ridley, Cloud, Incineroar.***
